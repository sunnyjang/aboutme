an optimist, a student at Penn, a daughter, and a photographer dedicated to capturing life’s most special moments.

When I was 8 I broke my first camera. While I was sad that my parents were furious, I was more upset that my family lost all the photos we took that day.

I saw the power in stopping to say ’cheese’ every so often.

Just like cheese is made by carefully curdling milk, I’ve learned to carefully care for my cameras. Throughout the past 10 years, I’ve been very lucky to travel the world and with that I’ve taken photos every step of the way.
